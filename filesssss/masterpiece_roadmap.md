This document contains the blueprint for turning this site into a **world-class, Awwwards-winning masterpiece**. It includes typography overhauls, pure-code WebGL animations (that require zero external assets), and highly cinematic video-synced interactions.

## 🖋️ 1. The Content & Typography Overhaul (The Soul of the Site)
Before we layer on the crazy effects, the actual *content* needs to hit hard. We will do a complete overhaul of:
*   **The Typography:** We will refine the exact font choices, weights, and massive scale contrasts. We need a perfect balance of brutalist huge text and delicate, cinematic micro-copy.
*   **The Copywriting:** We are rewriting the actual text. No generic portfolio words. It needs to read like a script for a dark, high-end film. It should be poetic, intense, and deeply atmospheric.
*   **Text Sizing & Hierarchy:** Dialing in exactly how the text sits on mobile vs PC so it feels like a magazine layout on every single screen size.

## 🔮 2. World-Class Animations (Pure Code — No Assets Needed)
These are mind-bending interactions I will build entirely from scratch using WebGL, Three.js, and advanced math. *I don't need any videos or images from you for these.*

*   **Volumetric Fluid & Smoke Simulation (WebGL):** I will code a real-time, procedural fluid simulation for the background. As you scroll or drag your finger on mobile, it physically parts dense, dark smoke that swirls and reacts to your velocity.
*   **Interactive Particle Swarms:** Thousands of tiny glowing sparks that live in the background. When you are idle, they slowly orbit your screen. When you swipe or scroll, they scatter violently like embers in the wind.
*   **Procedural Glass Refraction (GLSL):** I will write a custom shader that creates an invisible glass lens over the screen. It physically refracts and warps the typography and smoke behind it. Moving your thumb rotates the lens in 3D space.
*   **Magnetic Rigid-Body Typography:** The text on the screen becomes a live physics simulation. If you flick a word with your finger, the letters break apart, bounce off the edges of the phone screen, and magnetically snap back together.

---

## 🎬 3. Video & Asset-Driven Masterpieces
For effects that require specific, high-end cinematic assets, **I will always try to source or generate them myself first.** If I cannot find the absolute perfect shot (e.g., a specific drifting JDM car or a seamless rain-on-glass loop), I will give you the exact "Director's Vision" of what I need, and you can pull it from Pinterest.

*   **Video-Synced DOM Destruction:** We bind a high-speed car video to the scroll wheel. As the car drifts across the screen, it has a physical collision hitbox that *smashes* your website's text out of the way.
*   **Luma-Matte Ink Drop Transitions:** Using a black-and-white ink drop or glitch video as an alpha-mask. When you click a link, the new page is revealed *through* the spreading ink.
*   **The "Window to Another World" (Perspective Masking):** A deeply atmospheric video plays *inside* the massive typography on the screen. As you tilt your phone (gyroscope) or move your mouse, the perspective of the video inside the text shifts, like looking through a physical keyhole into another dimension.
