# 07 · The Story, Midnight, and Dossier Wings

These three wings are planned but not built. Story and Dossier are blocked on the owner's raw material.
Midnight can be drafted now from his known taste.

---

## The Story Wing .. `/story/[slug]` (4 chapters)

The four timeline sections on `/story` become clickable long form editorial chapters. This is where "write a
lot about everything" lives, governed hard by the Table Rule: depth through scenes and systems, never
confession. Data file to create: `src/data/chapters.ts`.

**Shared chapter anatomy (magazine, not blog):** cinematic header (huge ghosted chapter numeral, title,
years, one line thesis) → prose in 3 to 5 titled "movements" with drop caps → marginalia (small mono side
notes on desktop: dates, coordinates, dry one liners) → one enormous Cormorant italic pull quote per movement
→ optional artifact image bands (no repeat rule holds) → a single reserved closing line → prev/next.

**The four chapters and their scope:**
1. **The Iron Base** (the six school years, Jnana Prabodhini Prashala, 2019 to 2025). Scope: what that kind of
   school does to a kid, the independence, the drills, the quiet codes. Scenes: dorm mornings, the first time
   silence felt like an ally. Use the school ethos as atmosphere (see `03-owner-profile.md`), but keep the
   name off the page. NOT: names, incidents involving others, anything raw. CONFIRM: boarding vs day school.
2. **The Pressure Chamber** (the JEE grind). Scope: what pressure is when it is chosen, building the first
   personal systems, Pune at 2am. NOT: rank anxiety, family details.
3. **Proof of Concept** (the first builds). Scope: MEGADRESS then Vibe Link, the moment ideas stopped being
   theoretical. Cross links into the Arsenal pages (the wings connect). NOT: numbers he cannot prove.
4. **The Long Horizon** (now and forward). Scope: how the day is structured, the ten year frame, what
   compounding means to him. Deliberately the shortest chapter; the future stays mostly off the table, which
   is the point.

**Signature (one, shared across the wing):** the story rail (the animated signal line) continues onto chapter
pages and tracks reading progress down the margin, drawing as you read. Scroll linked SVG, transform only.

**What the owner owes (Phase D, the big blocker):** raw memory dumps per chapter, any length, unfiltered.
Voice notes are ideal. He dumps, we filter through the Table Rule together. School years especially: scenes,
textures, routines. Do not let him self censor at the dump stage; that is the filter's job later.

## The Midnight Wing .. `/midnight/[slug]` (3 car shrines)

The three car cards become clickable shrine pages: part love letter, part spec sheet, answering "why this car
specifically." Opinions on cars are personality, not vulnerability, so the voice gets to be loud here. Data
file to create: `src/data/cars.ts`. Can be drafted from taste now; no owner content required (a single line
of real conviction per car makes them his instead of generic).

**Shared anatomy:** full bleed hero (reuse the existing card image as the hero of its own page, counts as the
same placement upgraded) → "why this one" essay (4 to 6 paragraphs of pure taste) → the gauge cluster (specs
as a dashboard HUD: engine, power, weight, top speed, units built, dial styled mono numbers) → one closing
line → prev/next.

- **Pagani Huayra** .. art you can hear coming. Angle: theatricality and craft, active aero as choreography.
  Signature: spec dials sweep up from zero on scroll.
- **Bugatti Chiron** .. quiet menace. Angle: overwhelming force kept perfectly calm, the W16 as a dare,
  restraint as intimidation (the most NOCTURNE car philosophy). Signature: a 0 to 400 numerals strip that
  blurs past once.
- **Pagani Zonda** .. the masterpiece. Angle: the imperfect analog legend, the last of a kind over the newest
  thing, carbon weave as poetry. Signature: a carbon fiber weave texture band (CSS gradient, zero images).

## The Dossier .. `/dossier` (the interests page)

The "everything about what I like and want" page, built to read like a declassified file instead of a dating
profile. Files are factual, stamped, and partially redacted by nature, so mystery is baked into the format.
Data file to create: `src/data/dossier.ts`.

**Format:** one long page of stamped file sections, mono headers, paper thin hairlines. Some entries carry
literal redaction bars (solid rectangles that never reveal anything; hover gives a tooltip
`clearance insufficient`). Two or three redactions, placed for wit, never explained.

**Sections:**
- **ON RECORD** (provable things done): chess, swimming, volleyball, the 10k page, the shipped builds (cross
  link to Arsenal). Facts, no adjectives doing heavy lifting.
- **IN TRAINING** (the horizon): drift a car (top of the list), plus whatever the owner supplies. Each entry
  gets a status tag: `QUEUED` / `SCHEDULED` / `INEVITABLE`. The tags carry the confidence.
- **FREQUENCIES** (what he will talk about at 2am): systems, cars, AI, books, brand, how attention works. A
  tag cloud of mono chips. His four languages (English, Hindi, Marathi, German) fit here or in Coordinates.
- **STATIC** (what he will not): three or four entries, at least one fully redacted. Short. PR work disguised
  as personality.
- **COORDINATES** (taste, rapid fire): favorite hour (02:14), rain over sun, Batman canon opinions, reading
  pace, current obsession. One liners only.

**Signature:** each file section "declassifies" on scroll, a `REVIEWED · CLEARED FOR RELEASE` stamp fires
once. Nav placement: joins the main index, footer index updated, constellation gets its node.

**What the owner owes (Phase E):** the four raw lists (done / horizon / talk / won't) plus Coordinates
favorites and quirks.
