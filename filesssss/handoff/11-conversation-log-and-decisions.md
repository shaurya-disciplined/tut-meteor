- **v1** was the childish, oversharing first site. It got scrapped.
- **v2 (NOCTURNE)** rebuilt the whole thing: the design system, the persistent smoke, the atmosphere, the
  machine feel, the deep end, the restraint pass. All five phases shipped by 2026-07-07.
- **v3 ("The Depth Update")** was written as a plan only document (`NOCTURNE_DEPTH_v3.md`): turn every surface
  section into a page you can walk into, governed by the Table Rule and the Device Mercy Rule.
- **2026-07-09, this session:** the owner decided to execute v3. He chose the workflow "draft it all, then I
  red-pen," but asked for **deep research of his real footprint first** so the drafts are grounded in truth,
  not invention.
- **Research done:** GitHub (`shaurya-disciplined`) and the live Notrik and Vibe Link sites were readable.
  LinkedIn and Instagram were login gated (HTTP 999) and unreadable by tool; the owner later pasted his full
  LinkedIn manually. The school (Jnana Prabodhini Prashala) was researched from public sources.
- **Discoveries that changed the plan:** Notrik is a real AI study product (not just a coding sprint);
  AetherMem is the active flagship with real architecture (four memory tiers + a guarded self evolution loop);
  **ReachMonk** existed on GitHub but was missing from the site; the owner then introduced **HackWay**, a
  private AI hackathon startup, and provided its README plus screenshots.
- **The owner provided real screenshots** for five projects and his full LinkedIn, and asked that the
  screenshots be used to build striking page visuals that show what each product does, with copy explaining
  features and the origin idea, all under the no hyphen rule.
- **The Arsenal wing was built** (data file, detail route, clickable listing) with first draft copy.
- **The owner then asked for this handoff folder** to be created before any further building, so the full
  context survives a handoff to any future AI or person.
